Cars Made Better.

Hydro-Devil 4.5

Our car is Hydro powered to make sure that it is environmentally friendly. This will lead to reduced environmental footprint, improved fuel efficiency, reduced emission amongst many other improvements. Did you know, over 1,300,000 people worldwide die from road accidents per year? As a result, our car will include up-to-date safety features.

Automatic Braking
Multiple motion sensors and camera that detects movement around the car. AI then analyzes the potential of a crash and makes the decision to decelerate or stop turning within a single millisecond.

Airbags (Internal)
Internal airbags. Simple, but effective

Autopilot
The car will have a driver assistance system that will allow the car to steer, accelerate, and brake all on its own. Will allow driving to be easier and less accident prone.

Traffic Light Detection
Alongside autopilot, our cars will be able to detect the current state of traffic lights during manual driving, such as when they’re green or red.

How to Make the World Better

Issues About Carbon Emissions

The Hydro-Devil 4.5 is aimed to make transportation better, but you need to understand how to get there. Currently air pollution is primarily caused by the burning of fossil fuels in vehicles, industrial facilities, and power plants, etc. Car and truck exhaust is top five of the factors contributing to air pollution. The dangerous health effects from vehicle pollution including respiratory, and cardiovascular diseases, as well as increased risk of cancer. That is why we are determined to push our hydrogen powered car and to support electric cars as well.

These non hydrogen powered cars emit about three billion metric tons of carbon emissions, which cause a huge amount of pollution. Electric vehicles typically have a smaller carbon footprint than gasoline cars even when accounting for the electricity aspect used for charging plus they are way more efficient when it comes to energy use.
